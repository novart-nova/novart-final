Learn More About NovArt

What is NovArt?

NovArt is more than just a drawing app — it’s a creative space for everyone! Whether you’re a professional artist, a curious beginner, or a kid who loves colors, NovArt has tools made just for you. Our app combines professional-grade features with fun and simplicity to let everyone draw freely.

Who is it for?

  • Kids: Safe, playful, and full of fun coloring pages designed for young artists.
  • Beginners: Step-by-step tutorials and simple tools to help you grow your skills easily.
  • Professionals: A powerful set of tools for detailed drawing, layering, and more.

Why choose NovArt?

We believe that creativity should be accessible to everyone. That’s why NovArt is:

  • Easy to use with an intuitive interface.
  • Available on web — no need to download anything!
  • Includes AI drawing assistance to help you complete your artwork faster.
  • Offers both fun and learning in one place.

Let your imagination flow!

NovArt is your new favorite place to explore, draw, learn, and grow. Whether it’s your first time holding a digital pencil or you’re working on a masterpiece — we’re here to support your artistic journey.
